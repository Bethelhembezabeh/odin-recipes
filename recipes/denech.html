Simple Eggs Recipe

Description

Beth coming in with another Thebomb.com recipe for my potato lovers out there. This easy 4 ingredient recipe is simple and easy to make for breakfast, lunch and dinner; can be served as a snack, on its own, as a delicious sandwich or with rice. Lets get into it.

Ingredients

  • One diced Tomato
  • One medium sized diced Potato
  • 1/3 cup preferred cooking oil
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric

Steps

  1. Add oil and Tomato and Potato to a pan and cook for 15 min
  2. When the Tomato is well cooked, add the turmeric and stir well
  3. Let cook for 5 more minutes and serve (with salt if you like)
  4. Enjoy
