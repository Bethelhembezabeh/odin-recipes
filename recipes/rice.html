4-Ingredient Rice Recipe

Description

Do you love rice as much as me? Would you eat it every single meal if you could?

Then boy, do I have the just the thing for you! This recipe is simple, fast, does not require you to be a chef AND only has 4 ingredients. Yes, you heard that right folks 4! and thats including cooking oil. Here's how to make your new favorite food

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of preferred cooking oil
  • One Tomato diced
  • One serving of cooked Rice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Cut up Tomato (You can make the tomato slices as small as you like. The smaller, the more flavour)
  2. Put oil and Tomato in a pan and cook for 10 minutes on high heat
  3. Add cinnamon to your Tomato and stir well
  4. Lower the heat and at this point you can add salt if you prefer
  5. Add the rice and mix well
  6. Taste this delicious contraption and add more cinnamon if you like
  7. Enjoy
