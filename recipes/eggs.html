4-Ingredient Simple Eggs Recipe

Description

Who eats eggs for breakfast everyday? me ✋. So here is a 4-Ingredient egg recipe thats delicious, nutritious and instantaneous!

Ingredients

  • one diced Tomato
  • 1/4 of a diced bel pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • 2/5 cup preferred cooking oil

Steps

  1. Add oil and Tomato to a pan and cook for 15 min
  2. Beat the eggs while Tomatoes cook
  3. When 15 min is up, add the eggs and pepper together
  4. Stir until eggs are well cooked and turn off the stove
  5. Keep the pan closed with it's lid so that the pepper's aroma can get absorbed
  6. You can add salt if you like, but the dish is done and can be served
  7. Enjoy
